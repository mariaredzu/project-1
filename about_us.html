Nani

We are a company based in Madrid, our job is to transport animals from shelters to events of all kinds.

In Nani, we make sure to provide and care for the animals during said events, and work as a middle man betwen the shelters and anyone that wants to adopt them.

We encourage you to use our services, have fun with these beautiful creatures and maybe, even give them a chance for a better life.

Contact us now and have fun!
